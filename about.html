THE STORY // 072406

EST. 2026

love, RACHEL is a digital (and hopefully one day printed) magazine where girls in STEM meet the tactile world of high-fashion editorial.

Born from the intersection of code and creative expression, this project explores what it means to be a girl in STEM in the modern age. We believe that traditionally male dominated fields and feminine beauty aren't opposites, but are the same language.

Every issue, made strictly with html and cascading style sheets, is a deep dive into girls who are changing the game, documenting the journey of building something from 0 to 100. This isn't your average fashion magazine; it's the stories of all the inspiring and amazing girls in STEM.
